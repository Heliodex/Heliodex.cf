An update to how all the projects I'm working on are going. Probably will do this a few times yearly.

## TSBB

TSBB (Typing Simulator But Better), a typing game I've been working on alongside a few others and the sort-of succesor to [Typing Simulator](https://roblox.com/games/6739204347/) by S-GAMES, has been stuck in development hell for... sixteen months now.

It's essentially complete though (i promise), and all that is needed is a name for the project. Please give suggestions, as I have basically no ideas. "Typing Simulator" is already polluted with games in Roblox search results, and "TSBB" isn't catchy or descriptive.

## [eChat](https://github.com/Heliodex/eChat)

eChat, the un-blockable bookmarklet chat app, was recently blocked on school chromebooks and I've open sourced it at [Heliodex/eChat](https://github.com/Heliodex/eChat), [Heliodex/eChatWebsite](https://github.com/Heliodex/eChatWebsite), [Heliodex/eChatPopup](https://github.com/Heliodex/eChatPopup). I have no further plans to do anything with it, though I might be able to rework it into something similar to DocSocial.

I realised The CI/CD seems to have broken as well before it was blocked, so 1.1.5 is the latest version available.
